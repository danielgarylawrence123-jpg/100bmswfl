⬥ Join the Brotherhood

Membership

Connect with accomplished professionals committed to making a difference. Members mentor, lead programs, and participate in meaningful community service.

⬥ Why Join

Membership Benefits

Professional Network

Connect with accomplished professionals and community leaders across Southwest Florida and nationally through 110+ chapters.

Leadership Development

Access workshops, training programs, and hands-on leadership opportunities through chapter programs and national conferences.

Mentoring Impact

Directly shape the lives of young men and women through Bridge Builders, Collegiate 100, and Miss C100 programs.

National Conferences

Participate in annual 100 Black Men of America conferences alongside 10,000+ members nationwide.

Community Service

Civic engagement opportunities including the Drew Gala, Juneteenth celebration, Community Breakfasts, and health initiatives.

Professional Growth

Career advancement resources, entrepreneurship support, and financial literacy programming for members and mentees alike.

⬥ What It Takes

Requirements

01

Commitment

Dedication to the mission and values of 100 Black Men

02

Participation

Active involvement in chapter programs and events

03

Role Model

Serve as a positive example for youth and the community

04

Dues

Payment of annual membership dues

05

Background Check

Successful completion of a background screening

06

Brotherhood

Willingness to support and uplift fellow members
