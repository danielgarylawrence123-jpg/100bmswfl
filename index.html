⬥ Our Mission

Four for the Future.

We improve quality of life for African Americans and other minorities by developing leaders through four interlocking commitments.

01

Mentoring

One-on-one relationships that shape character. Bridge Builders runs through high school graduation.

02

Education

Tutoring, college readiness, and scholarships. Over $100,000 awarded to date.

03

Health & Wellness

Physical, mental, and emotional wellness for young men and their communities.

04

Economic Empowerment

Financial literacy, career pathways, and entrepreneurship.
