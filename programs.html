Our Programs

Six programs. One mission. Every initiative is designed to develop leaders, strengthen families, and uplift Southwest Florida.

06
Mentoring Education
Program 01 · Flagship

Bridge Builders
Leadership Academy

Our flagship mentoring program connects young men in grades 6 through 12 with positive role models for personal growth, academic success, and leadership development.

Bridge Builders meets monthly and runs through high school graduation. Mentors are trained through the national "Mentoring The 100 Way" curriculum, and every session focuses on real-world skills: schoolwork, career exploration, conflict resolution, and community service.

Currently running at Fort Myers Middle Academy in partnership with the Boys and Girls Club of SWFL.

Education Leadership
Program 02

Collegiate 100

A leadership development auxiliary for college-bound and enrolled students. Collegiate 100 members focus on academic excellence, professional development, and community service while receiving mentorship from active chapter members.

Established nationally in 1994 by 100 Black Men of America, the program bridges the gap between high school mentoring and professional life, creating a pipeline of future leaders.

Empowerment Education
Program 03

Miss C100

An empowerment program for young women that emphasizes education, leadership, and community engagement. Miss C100 teaches presentation skills, personal development, and civic responsibility through workshops, mentorship, and public speaking opportunities.

Participants develop confidence through structured programs including the Walk Like a Lady Challenge, voice training, presentation workshops, and technology lab sessions.

Program 04

Dr. Charles R. Drew
Excellence in
Medicine Gala

Our premier annual fundraising gala honoring excellence in medicine and healthcare leadership. The Drew Gala brings together medical professionals, community leaders, and supporters for an evening of recognition and philanthropy.

All proceeds support chapter programs including Bridge Builders, Collegiate 100, and scholarship awards.

Program 05

Juneteenth
Celebration

An annual community celebration commemorating freedom and African American heritage at the STARS Complex in Fort Myers. Four community organizations partner to create SWFL's premier Juneteenth gathering.

Features live entertainment, food vendors, educational programs, and family activities. Free and open to the public.

Program 06

Community
Breakfast

Monthly fellowship bringing together mentors, families, and community members. The breakfast features guest speakers, health and wellness conversations, networking, and the kind of connection that builds movements.

Open to the public. Focused on bringing men together to discuss mentoring, health, and economic opportunity in SWFL.
